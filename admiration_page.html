Why I Admire Him

Arijit Singh is not just a singer, he is an emotion for millions of people. His voice carries pain, love, and peace all at once.


From a small town in West Bengal to becoming India’s most loved playback singer, his journey is full of dedication, struggle, and passion for music.


Every song he sings feels real because he connects deeply with the lyrics. That is what makes him different from others.


Even after massive success, he remains humble and avoids unnecessary spotlight, focusing only on his music.
